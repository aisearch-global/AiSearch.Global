Our Story

A pure-play AEO specialist

Most digital agencies treat AI visibility as an add-on -- a line item bolted onto traditional SEO services. AISearch Global was built differently.

As a pure-play specialist in Answer Engine Optimisation, that focus matters because AEO requires a fundamentally different way of thinking about how content is structured, how entities are verified, and how AI systems decide what to trust.

Most AEO practitioners approach visibility as a purely technical problem. We approach it differently -- because it is not just a technical problem. It is a trust problem.

Understanding how those systems behave requires understanding both the machine logic and the human logic underneath it. That dual lens is what AISearch Global was built on.

Why it matters

Why human-centred AI training matters for AEO

AI systems do not recommend businesses arbitrarily. They synthesise patterns across thousands of sources to determine what is credible, clear, and useful enough to surface in an answer. That process mirrors how humans evaluate trust -- and understanding both sides of it produces better outcomes.

A psychology degree and formal study in AI empathy, ethics, and human-centred AI design are not standard credentials for a digital marketing consultant. At AISearch Global, they are central to the methodology -- because building AI visibility is ultimately about building machine-readable trust, and trust is a deeply human concept.

Since 2024, our founder has completed more than 40 certifications specifically in artificial intelligence, generative AI, prompt engineering, AI ethics, human-centred AI design, and responsible AI for mental health. That is not a collection of badges. It is a structured effort to understand how AI systems work, how they should be designed to serve people ethically, and how businesses can position themselves to be trusted and cited by those systems.
