AI Brand Presence

We strengthen the consistent entity signals across key authoritative sources that AI systems rely on to verify and recommend your brand.

What this covers

  • Entity consistency audit across directories, citations, and owned profiles
  • NAP (name, address, phone) alignment across all indexed sources
  • Third-party mention analysis and gap identification
  • Authoritative profile optimisation for AI-read sources

Why it matters

AI platforms cross-reference multiple sources before recommending a business. Inconsistent or sparse entity signals cause platforms like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews to default to competitors with cleaner data footprints.

Deliverables

  • Entity signal audit report
  • Prioritised source correction list
  • Ongoing monitoring baseline
