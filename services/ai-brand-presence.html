Frequently asked questions

What is AI brand presence?

AI brand presence refers to how consistently and accurately your business entity is represented across the authoritative sources that AI systems use to verify and recommend brands — including directories, review platforms, knowledge graphs, and citations. Strong entity signals help AI platforms confidently identify, describe, and recommend your business.

What does the AI Brand Presence service deliver?

Deliverables include an entity signal audit report across key indexed sources, a prioritised correction list with source-by-source instructions, and recommendations for strengthening brand presence in the data sources AI systems use as grounding references.

How is AI Brand Presence different from regular brand management?

Regular brand management focuses on visual identity, messaging, and customer perception. AI Brand Presence focuses on the structured data sources and entity signals that AI language models use when verifying and citing businesses — including schema markup, consistent NAP data, authoritative directory listings, and citation patterns.
