Frequently asked questions

What is schema markup and why does it matter for AEO?

Schema markup is structured JSON-LD code added to web pages that explicitly communicates your business identity, services, location, and content to AI systems and search engines. For AEO, schema markup is Layer 2 of the AEO Traction Stack and directly improves how AI platforms understand, verify, and cite your business in generated answers.

What schema types does AISearch Global implement?

AISearch Global implements schema types tailored to your business and service structure, including Organisation, LocalBusiness, ProfessionalService, Service, FAQPage, Article, BreadcrumbList, WebPage, and WebSite schema. All schema is written, validated, and delivered as production-ready JSON-LD.

Do I need schema markup for AEO?

Yes. Schema markup is one of the most direct signals available for improving AI citation readiness. It allows AI platforms to extract structured facts about your business without ambiguity, making it far more likely that your business is confidently cited in AI-generated answers.
