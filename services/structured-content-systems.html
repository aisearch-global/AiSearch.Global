Structured Content Systems

We build reusable page frameworks and editorial processes your team can use to consistently publish content that AI platforms can extract and cite.

What we build

  • Answer-ready page templates by content type (service, location, FAQ, case study)
  • Heading hierarchy and information architecture standards
  • Editorial checklists for AI extractability
  • Schema markup guidelines for in-house publishing teams

Why it matters

One-off page fixes create one-off results. A content system ensures every new page your team publishes meets the structural requirements AI platforms use to decide what to cite — without needing an external consultant each time.

Deliverables

  • Content template library (by page type)
  • Publishing checklist and style guide
  • Onboarding session for your content or marketing team
