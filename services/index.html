Full details →
Summary. Add production-ready JSON-LD so AI systems have cleaner machine-readable context for your business and services.

What it is

We deploy production-ready JSON-LD structured data aligned to your services, location, and entity footprint. Schema markup gives AI systems machine-readable context they can trust — reducing ambiguity and increasing the likelihood your business is cited.

Schema types we commonly deploy

  • Organization and ProfessionalService / LocalBusiness — your core entity identity
  • FAQPage — extracts Q&A pairs directly into AI answers
  • Service — maps each service offering to structured data
  • BreadcrumbList and WebPage — site structure and page context
  • Article and HowTo where content supports it

Deliverables

  • Production-ready JSON-LD implemented directly into your site
  • Validation report confirming schema is error-free
  • Documentation for your developer to maintain going forward

Best for: Businesses whose content is solid but whose site has no structured data, incomplete schema, or errors flagged in Google’s Rich Results Test. Often identified as a priority fix during an AI Visibility Audit.

Layer 1 — AEO Traction Stack

AI Brand Presence

AI systems verify your business before recommending it. Inconsistent data fails that check.

Enquire
Full details →
Summary. Align your business details across the web so AI systems see one consistent, trustworthy entity.

What it is

Before an AI platform recommends your business, it cross-checks your information across multiple sources — Google Business Profile, Facebook, LinkedIn, industry directories, review platforms. If your name, address, phone number, or service description varies between sources, AI systems treat your entity as unverified and move on to a competitor with cleaner data.

We audit and align your entity signals so AI systems see a consistent, trustworthy business at every source they check.

What we cover

  • Full entity consistency audit across Google, Facebook, LinkedIn, and key Australian directories
  • NAP (name, address, phone) alignment — even small inconsistencies like “Pty Ltd” vs “Pty. Ltd.” matter
  • Third-party mention analysis — where your business is cited, what it says, what’s missing
  • Authoritative profile optimisation on sources AI systems weight most highly

Deliverables

  • Entity signal audit report
  • Prioritised source correction list with specific fixes
  • Monitoring baseline so you can track changes yourself going forward

Best for: Businesses that have moved premises, rebranded, or accumulated listings across directories over years — and any business that has never done a systematic check of their external data footprint.
