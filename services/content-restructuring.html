Content Restructuring

Your existing pages probably contain the right information — they're just not structured in a way AI systems can extract and cite. We fix that without starting from scratch.

How does content restructuring improve AI visibility?

We audit your existing pages and rewrite them into formats AI systems can parse, extract, and attribute. The focus is structural: question-led headings, short definitive answer blocks beneath each one, clear entity context throughout, and the three Princeton GEO content signals proven to lift AI citation rates by up to 40% — statistics, source citations, and quotations. We don't change your message — we make it machine-readable and citation-ready.

What’s included

  • Page-by-page AI extractability audit
  • Question-led H2/H3 heading rewrites
  • Short, direct answer paragraphs beneath each heading
  • Entity and service context — who you are, what you do, where you operate
  • Princeton GEO content signals applied — statistics, source citations, and quotations woven in where relevant (peer-reviewed tactics from the KDD 2024 study across 10,000 AI queries)
  • Internal linking review for topical depth
  • Schema alignment check on restructured pages

Who this is for

Businesses with existing content — service pages, about pages, location pages — that aren't being cited in AI answers. Works best after an AI Visibility Audit has identified which pages have the most opportunity. Not a fit for businesses that need content written from scratch.
