About this tool

The AISearch Global AEO Score Calculator is a free tool built by AISearch Global, Australia's pure-play Answer Engine Optimisation consultancy based in Sydney. Updated with the Princeton GEO Framework integrated, it now analyses 20 signals to give business owners the most complete AI visibility read available in a free tool.

Unlike traditional SEO audits, this tool focuses specifically on the signals that AI systems — ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude, DeepSeek, and Grok — use when deciding which businesses to cite in generated answers. The score combines structural AEO signals (machine-readability, entity clarity, schema markup) with the 3 peer-reviewed Princeton GEO content tactics proven to boost AI citation visibility — statistics, source citations, and quotations.

For a full analysis with a prioritised 90-day action plan specific to your business and industry, see the AISearch Global AI Visibility Audit.
