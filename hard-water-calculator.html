Why only 92 postcodes?

Every figure here traces to a water utility's own published reporting, with a retrieval date. We'd rather cover fewer areas honestly than pad the list with guesses — postcodes we haven't verified are matched to the nearest confirmed supply zone and flagged as an estimate.
