FAQ

Common AEO questions

What is AEO?

AEO improves your likelihood of being used and cited in AI-generated answers by improving structure, clarity, and trust signals.

Do I still need SEO?

Yes. SEO and AEO are complementary. Strong search fundamentals support discoverability and authority, while AEO improves answer inclusion.

How quickly can results appear?

Some improvements can appear within weeks, but sustainable gains typically require consistent optimisation and monitoring over several months.
