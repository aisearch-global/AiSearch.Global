FAQ

Common AI visibility questions

What is AI Search Optimization?

AI Search Optimization helps businesses appear in AI-generated answers across platforms like ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Gemini, and Perplexity.

How is AEO different from traditional SEO?

Traditional SEO focuses on rankings in search engines. AEO focuses on improving how AI systems understand, reference, and recommend your business in generated answers.

Can my business appear in ChatGPT or Google AI Overviews?

Yes. Businesses with strong content structure, authority signals, semantic relevance, and clear information architecture are more likely to be referenced by AI systems.

What industries do you work with?

We work with service businesses, trades, construction, roofing, healthcare, professional services, eCommerce, and growing brands looking to improve AI visibility.

What is included in an AI Visibility Audit?

Our audit reviews your AI search presence, competitor visibility, structured content, schema implementation, citation opportunities, and AI-readiness across major AI platforms.

How long does it take to see results?

AI visibility improvements can begin within weeks, but meaningful authority and citation growth typically develop over several months depending on competition and website quality.

Do I need to redesign my website?

Not necessarily. In many cases, strategic content restructuring, schema improvements, FAQ optimization, and semantic clarity can significantly improve AI discoverability.

Why does AI visibility matter now?

Search behavior is rapidly shifting toward AI-generated answers. Businesses that adapt early are more likely to become trusted sources within emerging AI search ecosystems.
