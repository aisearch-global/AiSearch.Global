FAQ

Common AI visibility questions

What is AI Search Optimization?

AI Search Optimization helps businesses appear in AI-generated answers across platforms like ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude, DeepSeek, and Grok. It involves structuring content, schema, and entity signals so AI systems can clearly identify, verify, and cite your business when answering relevant queries.

How is AEO different from traditional SEO?

Traditional SEO focuses on rankings in search engines. AEO focuses on improving how AI systems understand, reference, and recommend your business in generated answers. Where SEO optimises for crawler indexing, AEO optimises for AI extractability — ensuring your content is machine-readable enough to be cited directly in synthesised answers.

Can my business appear in ChatGPT, Claude, or Google AI Overviews?

Yes. Businesses with strong content structure, authority signals, semantic relevance, and clear information architecture are more likely to be referenced by AI systems. The key is making your web presence machine-readable so AI platforms can confidently extract and cite your business details without ambiguity.

What industries do you work with?

We work with service businesses, trades, construction, roofing, healthcare, professional services, eCommerce, and growing brands looking to improve AI visibility. Any business competing for commercial clients through online discovery can benefit from improved AI citation outcomes.

What is included in an AI Visibility Audit?

Our audit reviews your AI search presence, competitor visibility, structured content, schema implementation, citation opportunities, and AI-readiness across major AI platforms including ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, DeepSeek, and Grok. You receive a prioritised 90-day roadmap with specific fixes ranked by impact.

How long does it take to see results?

AI visibility improvements can begin within weeks, but meaningful authority and citation growth typically develop over several months depending on competition and website quality. Schema fixes and content restructuring tend to show the fastest gains; entity authority builds gradually with consistent, structured publication.

Do I need to redesign my website?

Not necessarily. In many cases, strategic content restructuring, schema improvements, FAQ optimisation, and semantic clarity can significantly improve AI discoverability without a full redesign. Most improvements happen at the content and structured data layer, not the visual design layer.

Why does AI visibility matter now?

Search behaviour is rapidly shifting toward AI-generated answers. Businesses that adapt early are more likely to become trusted sources within emerging AI search ecosystems including ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude, DeepSeek, and Grok. Early movers gain durable citation authority before their category becomes competitive in AI search.
